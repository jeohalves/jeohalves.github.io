Biography

Jeovane Honório Alves is a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Federal University of Parana (UFPR). Research and professional interests are artificial intelligence (AI), computer vision (CV), machine learning (ML), deep learning (DL), neural architecture search (NAS), evolutionary computation, image processing, natural language processing (NLP), and time series analysis. His thesis is focused on ML, CV and NAS applied to natural and medical images (e.g., CIFAR, ImageNet, and CHAOS challenge datasets). He also worked with several frameworks, like PyTorch, Keras, SciKit-Learn, ITK, Keras, Pandas, Detectron, NumPy, SciPy, and OpenCV, for different tasks, like image classification, object detection, segmentation, regression, time series forecasting, and clustering.
